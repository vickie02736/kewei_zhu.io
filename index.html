About me

I'm a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Peyman Z Moghadam and Prof. Mojtaba Abdi-Jalebi at University College London (UCL) since 2023.

I am generally interested in applying machine learning to support scientific research in molecular simulation and data mining:

  • Development of emulation / surrogate models for complex simulations
  • Implementing large language models for text-mining from scientific literature to aid in data-driven discovery
