About me

I'm a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Peyman Z Moghadam and Prof. Mojtaba Abdi-Jalebi at University College London (UCL) since 2023.

I am passionate about applying machine learning to advance molecular research, especially in Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs). My research focuses on leveraging advanced AI techniques to accelerate scientific discovery, with emphasis on:

  • large language models and their applications, such as text mining, multimodal pretraining, and post-training;
  • conditional generative models, including LLaDA-series, flow matching, diffusion, VAE, and GAN frameworks;
  • machine learning potentials for molecular simulations;
  • applications of time-series models in computational fluid dynamics (CFD)
