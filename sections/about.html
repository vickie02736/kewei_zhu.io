About me

I'm a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Peyman Z Moghadam and Prof. Mojtaba Abdi-Jalebi at University College London (UCL) since 2023.

I am passionate about applying machine learning to advance molecular research, especially in Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs). My focus includes:

  • Chemical representation and QSPR modeling: Developing chemical embeddings and predictive models to understand and accelerate structure-property relationships.
  • Generative and multimodal AI for material design: Leveraging diffusion models and large language models (LLMs) to create and interpret novel materials across multiple data modalities.
